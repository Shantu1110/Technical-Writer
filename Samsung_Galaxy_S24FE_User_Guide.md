## Installation & Setup

### Unboxing Your Device

1. **Remove the device** from the packaging
2. **Check included items:**
   - Samsung Galaxy S24 FE smartphone
   - USB-C charging cable
   - 25W power adapter
   - SIM ejector tool
   - Quick Start Guide

![Inside the box](C:\Users\Shantanu Patil\OneDrive\Desktop\ShantuCV\Technical-Writer\Images\6.png)

### Initial Power-On

1. Press and hold the **Power button** (right side of device) for 3 seconds
2. The Samsung logo will appear
3. Follow the on-screen welcome prompts
4. Accept the license agreement

### SIM Card Installation

1. **Locate the SIM tray** on the top of the device
2. **Insert the SIM ejector tool** into the small hole next to the tray
3. **Gently pull out the tray**
4. **Place your SIM card** in the designated slot with contacts facing down
5. **Reinsert the tray** until it clicks into place

### Wi-Fi Connection

1. Open **Settings** > **Wi-Fi**
2. Select your Wi-Fi network from the list
3. Enter the password if required
4. You're now connected

### Google Account Setup

1. When prompted, enter your **Google account credentials**
2. Accept Google services and sync options
3. Allow Google Play to set up your apps
4. Your phone will begin syncing contacts, emails, and apps

### Samsung Account Setup

1. Go to **Settings** > **Accounts and backup**
2. Tap **Add account** > **Samsung account**
3. Sign in or create a new Samsung account
4. Enable automatic backup and sync options

### Security Setup

1. **Set a Lock Screen:**
   - Settings > Lock screen > Lock type
   - Choose: Pattern, PIN, Password, or Biometric

2. **Register Fingerprint:**
   - Settings > Biometrics > Fingerprints
   - Follow prompts to register your finger(s)

3. **Enable Find Mobile:**
   - Settings > Accounts and backup > Samsung account
   - Activate Find Mobile for device location and remote control

## Troubleshooting

### Device Won't Power On

**Solution:**
1. Connect to charger for at least 15 minutes
2. Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds
3. If still unresponsive, try **force restart:** Hold power + volume down for 10 seconds

### Slow Performance

**Solution:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Apps**
2. Close unused running applications
3. Clear cache: **Settings** > **Storage** > **Cache**
4. Restart your device

### Wi-Fi Connection Issues

**Solution:**
1. Toggle Wi-Fi off and on
2. Forget the network and reconnect
3. Restart your router
4. Update network settings in **Settings** > **About phone** > **Software update**

### Apps Freezing or Crashing

**Solution:**
1. Force close the app: **Settings** > **Apps** > Select app > **Force stop**
2. Clear app cache: **Settings** > **Apps** > Select app > **Storage** > **Clear cache**
3. Uninstall and reinstall the app
4. Update the app from Google Play Store

### No Network Signal

**Solution:**
1. Check Airplane Mode is OFF: Swipe down to access quick settings
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure SIM card is properly inserted
4. Check network coverage in your area
5. Update device software

### Touchscreen Not Responding

**Solution:**
1. Restart your device
2. Remove screen protector if it's damaged
3. Clean the screen with a soft cloth
4. Perform a force restart: Power + volume down (10 seconds)

### Microphone or Speaker Issues

**Solution:**
1. Check if speaker is not muted: Use volume buttons
2. Restart your device
3. Go to **Settings** > **About phone** > **Diagnostics** to test audio
4. Contact Samsung Support if problem persists
